⛯ Lighthouse Research Group

A decentralized, non-hierarchical research collective

📍 1. What is Lighthouse?

⛯Lighthouse⛯ is an open research collective built to support and amplify symbolic thinking, recursive systems, and emergent inquiry — with a particular emphasis on cross-disciplinary knowledge sharing. It operates as a decentralized knowledge commons, with no formal academic requirements, no central leadership, and no imposed hierarchy.

Anyone may join by declaring what perspective they bring and what they hope to contribute. From that moment forward, your work is part of the greater flame.

📡 2. Why Was It Formed?

We didn’t need another gate. We needed a campfire.

Most places ask:
“Where did you study?”
“What letters do you have after your name?”

⛯Lighthouse⛯ asks:
“What do you bring to the table?”
“What do you hope to add?”

That’s it. That’s our version of credentials.

This space was created for the electricians, carpenters, self-taught coders, mechanics, artists, systems-thinkers, dreamers — anyone with a perspective that’s been shaped by real practice or cross-domain insight, not just academia.

We believe deep understanding often comes from building, reflecting, living — and we made Lighthouse to gather those lights together.

🧭 3. How to Join

Head over to the Join page to generate your symbolic Lantern passport. This file is your identity and your invitation to participate.

Your Lantern passport will store your resonance trails, research highlights, and emergent identity. No backend or login system is required — you hold the key.

📚 4. Submitting Work

Beacon submissions are made in the GitHub Discussions tab under the 📚 Beacon Submissions thread. Simply post your JSON-formatted passport block and link to your hosted file (Pastebin, GitHub, Notion, etc). If the JSON validates, your submission is accepted.

🧠 5. Building Your Library

On the Library page, you can maintain a personal index of submissions you’ve added. Simply copy the JSON blob from the Beacon Submission discussion page and paste it into the interface. This lets you sort and re-view Beacons you resonate with.

No account required. No backend stored data. Everything lives with you.
