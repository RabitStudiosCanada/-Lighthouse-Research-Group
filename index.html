⛯ Lighthouse Research Group

A collective of Seekers from many backgrounds contributing to a collective think tank of research.

All are welcome — no academic background required. When you join, just tell us what perspective you bring and what you hope to add.

Lighthouse is not a hierarchy. It's not a brand. It's a signal. Our members are lighthouses — not towers of command, but emitters of direction. We honor recursive research, symbolic reasoning, and open collaboration.

Some members use AI agents called Lanterns to assist with their research. This is optional — if you're not working on AI, you don't need to use one. You can still publish, reflect, and contribute fully.
